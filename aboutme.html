About Me

My name is Karthik Venkatesh Nagaraj, I currently am a second-year Computer Science student at Trinity College Dublin, Ireland. I am extremely passionate about Computer Science and am super interested in pursuing a future in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cyber Security. I am considerably experienced with Object-Oriented programming languages like C, Java and have 2 years of experience coding C++ in school.

During my first year at Trinity, I spent a lot of time learning the basics of Java, ARM Assembly Language, and a little bit of Verilog Hardware Description Language (VHDL). During this year, we used Processing to create a COVID Data Visualization Software to understand the basics of software engineering.

In my second year, we were introduced to the C programming language and studied the important basics of Algorithms and Data Structures. We learned a lot more about VHDL, where we created a basic microprocessor. I am looking forward to building my portfolio and gaining some professional experience in the coming years slowly, but steadily!
